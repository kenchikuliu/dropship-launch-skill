# 7-Day Test Plan

Use this file when the user wants a first validation plan.

Adapt currency, target CPA, and price anchors to the chosen market.

## Budget

- total first test: local equivalent of `GBP 210` to `GBP 315`
- daily budget: local equivalent of `GBP 10` to `GBP 15` per ad set

If budget is tight, launch one product only.

## Default Campaign Setup

- objective: sales
- placement: broad placements are fine for first pass
- optimization: purchase

## Price Anchors

- hidden drawer: local equivalent of `GBP 29.99`
- under-sink organizer: local equivalent of `GBP 34.99`
- pet hair remover: local equivalent of `GBP 24.99`

## Metrics

Practical targets:

- link CTR above `1.5%`
- CPC below the local equivalent of `GBP 1.20`
- add-to-cart rate above `4%`
- checkout rate above `2%`

## Kill Rules

Kill a creative if:

- spend reaches about one selling price with no add-to-cart
- CTR is weak after enough impressions
- comments show a trust or product issue

## Scale Rules

Scale only when:

- at least 2 purchases exist
- CPA is workable
- comments are healthy

Increase budget by `20%` to `30%`, not a sudden jump.
