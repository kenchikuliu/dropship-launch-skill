# Ops Checklist

Use this file when preparing launch or diagnosing failure.

## Before Launch

- confirm supplier shipping to the chosen market
- confirm dimensions and packaging
- confirm refunds and returns policy
- confirm tracking events
- confirm mobile checkout works
- confirm no risky product claims are on the page
- confirm market-specific payment and tax assumptions

## Product Validation

- obvious problem
- obvious demo
- simple shipping
- low support burden
- low claim risk

## If Product One Fails

- stop spending quickly
- keep the store shell
- move to product two
- reuse the ad structure

## If Product One Shows Signal

- improve the page first
- tighten sourcing next
- consider bundles only after base conversion is real
