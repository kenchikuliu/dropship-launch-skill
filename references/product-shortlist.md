# Product Shortlist

Use this file when the user has not committed to a product yet.

## Product Traits To Prioritize

- clear problem
- easy demo
- low claim risk
- low fitment risk
- low breakage risk
- supports paid traffic margin

## First-Wave Shortlist

### Primary Launch Candidates

1. Under-desk hidden drawer
2. Pull-out under-sink organizer
3. Reusable pet hair remover roller

### Backup Candidates

1. Desk cable organizer kit
2. Bedside storage caddy
3. Drawer organizer set
4. Over-door hook rack
5. Mop and broom wall organizer

## Why These Work

- strong before/after visuals
- easy short-form demos
- broad household demand
- safer for first validation than regulated or complex items

## Scoring Rules

Score each candidate from 1 to 5 on:

- problem clarity
- demo strength
- shipping simplicity
- breakage risk
- refund risk
- ad-friendliness

Reject products that fail on:

- complex compatibility
- heavy or awkward shipping
- fragile construction
- regulated or claim-heavy marketing

## Suggested Price Positioning

Adjust by market, but use these as rough anchors:

- small utility item: local equivalent of `GBP 24.99` to `GBP 29.99`
- stronger organizer SKU: local equivalent of `GBP 29.99` to `GBP 39.99`

## Default Recommendation

If the user asks "what should I launch first," start with:

`under-desk hidden drawer`

Reason:

- easy to explain
- clear office/home use crossover
- simple single-purpose offer
